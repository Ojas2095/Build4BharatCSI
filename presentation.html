Problem Statement
What problem are we solving?
The Pradhan Mantri Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana (PMDDKY) targets 100 backward agricultural districts across India — Uttarakhand has 13 such districts.
Scheme administrators have no unified, real-time dashboard to monitor fund disbursement, beneficiary verification, and district progress simultaneously.
Data is scattered across paper-based registers and siloed spreadsheets, making audits slow and error-prone.
Who is affected?
District Collectors & Block Development Officers managing thousands of farmers across Uttarakhand
Policymakers at the state level who need accurate, real-time numbers to make funding decisions
Millions of rural farming beneficiaries whose welfare depends on timely & accurate fund disbursement
Why is it important?
Delays in fund release lead to crop failure, farmer distress, and reduced agricultural productivity
📊 Over 5.8 lakh farmers in Uttarakhand are eligible under PMDDKY — visibility gap is critical

Proposed Solution
📡 A web-based PMDDKY Smart Governance Platform — a real-time monitoring, GIS-mapping, and AI-analytics dashboard for all 13 Uttarakhand districts.
Key Features
Live KPI Dashboard — Animated real-time stats: total beneficiaries, funds disbursed, districts active, % progress
GIS District Map — Interactive Leaflet map of Uttarakhand with color-coded beneficiary density per district; click-to-explore popups
Beneficiary Management — Searchable, filterable table with Aadhaar-verified records, status badges (Verified / Pending / Rejected)
AI-Powered Analytics — Anomaly detection alerts, year-over-year trend charts, predictive fund allocation, district performance ranking
Real-Time Updates — Socket.IO live data push; dashboard auto-refreshes without page reload

How It Works (3 steps)
Step 1 — Ingest: District officials enter or update beneficiary status via the Beneficiary Management portal; data flows to Express REST API
Step 2 — Analyze: AI analytics engine computes district-wise efficiency scores, detects anomalies (unusual fund spikes), forecasts enrollment trends
Step 3 — Visualize: React frontend renders live charts, GIS choropleth maps, and activity feeds — all auto-updated via Socket.IO without page refresh

Feasibility & Innovation
Why It's Practical & Low-Cost
100% open-source stack — React, Node.js, Leaflet, Socket.IO — zero licensing costs
Deployable on any cloud — Works on ₹500/month VPS or government NIC servers; no expensive GPU required
Fully built in a hackathon window — Working prototype with real Uttarakhand district GeoJSON data, 500+ mock beneficiary records
Scalable architecture — Socket.IO server and REST APIs can handle district-level concurrent users; state-wide rollout to 100 districts is plug-and-play
What Makes It Innovative
🗺️ First GIS-mapped PMDDKY dashboard for Uttarakhand — district-level choropleth with live beneficiary density
🤖 AI anomaly detection — flags unusual fund spikes or enrollment drops before they become crises
⚡ Real-time Socket.IO updates — no refresh needed; field officers and HQ see data change live
📱 Responsive design — works on tablets used in District Collector offices as well as mobile phones for field workers
